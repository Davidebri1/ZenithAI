## Description (App Store / Google Play)

Send one prompt. Get every answer.

Zenith sends your question to all leading AI models simultaneously — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Grok, DeepSeek, Mistral, Llama, and Qwen — and streams their responses side by side in real time. No more switching apps. No more copy-pasting. One screen, every perspective.

**SYNTHESIS — THE KILLER FEATURE**
After the AIs respond, tap Synthesize. Zenith reads every answer and distills a single consensus summary across all models. Get the signal, not the noise.

**HOW IT WORKS**
1. Type your question
2. All AI Models respond simultaneously in real time
3. Tap Synthesize for the cross-model consensus
4. Tap any card to continue the conversation with that AI directly

**WHAT YOU GET**
• One prompt to All AI Models — simultaneously
• Synthesis — AI-powered consensus distilled across all responses
• Full conversation threads — go deeper with any individual AI
• Session history — every comparison saved locally for fast access
• Clean, cinematic design built for daily use

**MODELS INCLUDED**
ChatGPT (OpenAI) · Claude (Anthropic) · Gemini (Google) · Grok (xAI) · DeepSeek · Mistral · Llama (Meta) · Qwen (Alibaba)

**FREE & PRO**
Free plan: 10 prompts to try the full experience
Pro: Unlimited prompts across All AI Models
